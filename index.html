About us

Services

In addition to our medical and surgical services, we are fully equipped with the latest in dental equipment, including digital dental radiography. We offer routine cleanings (prophylaxis) and dental extractions.

We offer vaccination programs consistent with the American Association of Animal Hospital (AAHA) vaccine guidelines. We carry a range of medications to prevent intestinal worms, heartworm, and external parasites such as fleas, ticks and mange.

We provide surgical services ranging from routine spays and neuters to more complex bladder, ear, eye, gastrointestinal, orthopedic and tumour removal procedures. Our veterinarians are experienced surgeons, and we can call upon the services of a mobile specialist if orthopaedic work is needed. We also provide soft tissue surgery and tonometry procedure as well.

We have an in-house laboratory and access to off-site veterinary laboratories to complete any necessary testing for your loved one. Our other service includes medical assessments of your pets.

We offer inpatient and outpatient medical treatment for eye, ear, skin, gastrointestinal, respiratory, cardiac, urogenital, renal and hepatic diseases.
